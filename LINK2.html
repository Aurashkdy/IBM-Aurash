KING PRITHVI NARAYAN SHAH


AI Overview King Prithvi Narayan Shah (1723–1775) was the monarch of Gorkha who successfully unified the politically fragmented principalities into the modern, independent Kingdom of Nepal. Ascending the throne in 1743, he combined strategic warfare and diplomacy to conquer the Kathmandu Valley in 1769, establishing Kathmandu as the capital. His visionary leadership safeguarded the region from British colonial expansion.
